Company Profile

We do not list or sell real estate so our staff is completely focused on providing quality customer service to our property owners and their tenants.

Miller Property Solutions, Inc., doing business as Lee Property Management, is a privately held corporation specializing in the management of upscale homes, townhomes, and condos. We operate in serveral Hampton Roads areas including Yorktown, Williamsburg, Newport News, and Hampton.

The company was founded in 1994 by Charles R. Lee. In 2008, Mr. Lee retired and sold the business to longtime employee, Carol Miller. Lee Property Management currently manages approximately 170 single family residences in both covenant protected and non-covenant protected neighborhoods.
